A floral company dedicated to bringing fresh or presered bouquets to you. Based in San Luis Obispo, The Flower Project aims to provide qulaiity bouquets to peoples doors.

The consumer base of The Flower Project will range from event planners, to brides, and to anyone who wants to send a beautiful floral arrangemnt.

We hope to work with wedding venues and provide floral arrangments for recpetions and dinners. Creating fresh displays for any event or party. Addionally we have a floral delivery service to get these bouquets to any local address.
